Inbound SMS: Interacting with an Inbound SMS Campaign¶

Inbound messaging starts with an individual sending a trigger term to a program’s number.
This will enroll them in the SMS campaign’s workflow.
If this individual has an unknown phone number (meaning it is not associated with any contact in the Maha Platform), the Caller Identification service will search for a name to create a new contact.
Once an inbound SMS campaign is activated, it will remain active until it is deactivated.

  1. Click in the ribbon

  2. Click Campaigns

  3. Click Inbound SMS

  4. Click on an inbound SMS campaign to view details, an audit trail, and comments

  5. Click and Deactivate Campaign or Activate Campaign to change the status of the campaign

  6. Click , Delete Campaign, and Yes to delete the campaign along with all associated workflows and performance data

  7. Click Design Workflow to design your inbound SMS campaign using the blocks below:

    • Send Message , Ask Question , If/Then , Wait , Set Goal , Set Variable , Time of Day , Update Property , Update Consent , Update Lists , Update Topics , Enroll in Workflow , Send Email , Send SMS , Send Internal Email , Send Internal SMS

  8. Click Emails to click on the emails associated with the SMS campaign

  9. Click Performance to view recorded recipient interaction data
