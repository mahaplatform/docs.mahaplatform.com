New Inbound SMS Campaign¶

  1. Click in the ribbon

  2. Click Campaigns

  3. Click Inbound SMS

  4. Click the in the upper right hand corner

  5. Choose a program

  6. Enter a campaign title

  7. Enter a trigger term (contact sends a word –> sms campaign)

  8. Click Save
