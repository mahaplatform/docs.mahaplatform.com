Email CampaignsÂ¶

Email campaigns allow you to reach out to many contacts with one email. You have the ability to make your email and interactions as complex or simple as needed by designing your email and/or creating a workflow.
You email campaign will be in draft status when first created. You will have a few features available to you before the draft is sent, as indicated below.

  1. Click the in the ribbon

  2. Click Campaigns

  3. Click Email

  4. Click an email to view email details, an audit trail, and comments

  5. Click Automation and click on an existing workflow or click Create Workflow

  6. Click Performance to view recorded recipient interaction data

  7. Click View Email to open a web browser version of the email campaign

  8. (For email campaigns in draft mode) Click to click one of the options below to perform the associated action:

    • Edit Campaign: You can edit your email campign before sending it. When satisfied, click Save

    • Design Email: You can customize your email using blocks

    • Send/Schedule Campaign* You can curate a list of recipients under To (options detailed here `filter ). You can also opt to Schedule Delivery, by selecting a date and entering a time. Click Send

    • Convert to Template: Your email campaign will be available as a template . Enter the template title and click Save

    • Clone Campaign: Your email campaign will be cloned. You can edit the original email or leave all details identical. When satisfied, click Save

    • Delete Campaign: Your email (and all associated workflows and performance data) will be deleted. Click Yes

  9. (For email campaigns that have been sent) Click to click one of the options below to perform the associated action:

    • Convert to Template: Your email campaign will be available as a template . Enter the template title and click Save

    • Resend Campaign: Your email campaign will be resent, by default to the same recipients and immediately after you click Resend. You can change the list of contacts under To (options detailed here `filter ). You can also opt to Schedule Delivery, by selecting a date and entering a time

    • Clone Campaign: Your email campaign will be cloned. You can edit the original email or leave all details identical. When satisfied, click Save

    • Delete Campaign: Your email (and all associated workflows and performance data) will be deleted. Click Yes
