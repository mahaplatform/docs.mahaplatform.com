Stores: Managing a CampaignÂ¶

Campaigns allows you to communicate with your customers with customized messages via email, text message, or phone call.
You can create these campaigns and view and manage existing campaigns.
To manage the campaigns of a store, you will need to have navigated to the store. Refer here if you are having trouble reaching this point.

  1. Click Campaigns

  2. Click Email , SMS , or Voice to view the associated campaigns created for the store

  3. Click to create a new campaign (email , SMS , or voice )

  4. Click on a campaign to view the campaign details, automation, and performance (click the hyperlinks from step #2 to navigate to additional guides if you find any part confusing)
