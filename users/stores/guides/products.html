Stores: Creating and Managing the Products of a StoreÂ¶

To create and manage the products of a store, you should have navigated to the store. Refer here if you are having trouble reaching this point.

  1. Click Products

  2. Click and Deactivate Product or Activate Product to perform the associated action

  3. Click , Delete Product, and Yes to delete the product

  4. Click to create a new product

  5. Enter a title

  6. Select the category or categories to which the product will belong

  7. Select if the product is a Physical Product, a File, or a URL

  8. Enter a product description

  9. Click Next

  10. Select if there is only one variant of the product or if there are multiple variants of the product (click hyperlink for a guide to adding product variants)

  11. Click Next

  12. Select if you would like to use the same or different photos for each variant (ignore this step if you did not add variants)

    • Click + Add Photo(s) and upload the desired photos

    • RETURN WHEN GREG HAS FIXED THE VARIANT PHOTO BUG

  13. Click Next

  14. Select an inventory option:

    • Do not manage inventory: Select if you will not run out of a product or if the product supply is infinite

    • Stop selling when inventory reaches 0: Select if the product supply is finite

    • Allow sales to continue into negative inventory levels: Select if the supply is finite but you are able to replenish the supply as needed

  15. Click Next

  16. Complete the pricing of the product (click the hyperlink for a guide to pricing the product and, if applicable, its variants)

  17. Click Next

  18. Select a shipping strategy for the product (click the hyperlink for a guide to shipping of the product and, if applicable, its variants)

  19. Click Save
