Creating a Store¶

fdghj
To create a store, you must have a bank account linked to the program to which you plan to connect your store.

  1. Click the in the ribbon

  2. Click Stores

  3. Click the in the upper right hand corner

  4. Select a program to which you want to connect your store

  5. Enter a store title

  6. Enter a URL for the store

  7. Choose a cart life (after this number of hours has lapsed, an item in a customer’s cart is “abandonned” and returned to stock???)

  8. You may enable the options to pay by check or to pay later

  9. Choose if the store will always be open or if it will open at a specific time

    • For the second option, enter a date and time. Additionally, you can display a message or redirect to a URL when customers visit your store before it has opened

  10. Choose if the store will be open indefinitely or if it will close at a specific time

    • For the second option, enter a date and time. Additionally, you can display a message or redirect to a URL when customers visit your store after it has closed

  11. Enter the contact information of the person customers will be instructed to contact

    • Enter their First Name

    • Enter their Last Name

    • Enter their Email

    • Click Add field and select an existing contact field or create a custom contact field

  12. Create a confirmation email

    • Choose a sender

    • Enter the email addess customers will write to if they choose to reply to their confirmation email

    • Enter an email subject

    • Select an existing email template or create a new template

  1. To go back and edit information, click Prev, and to return click Next

  2. Click Save
