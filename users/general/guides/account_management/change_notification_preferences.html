Changing Notification PreferencesÂ¶

The notification preferences available to users are automatically turned on. To change your preferences, you will disable specific notifications or options, by following these steps.

  1. Click on your profile (a circle with your initials or profile picture) located in the ribbon

  2. Click Manage Notifications

  3. Click Change Preferences

  4. Disable all notifications by clicking Notifications are enabled

  5. Disable in-app notifications by clicking Display in-app notifications

  6. Disable notification sounds by clicking Play the Ding sound when I get a notification

  7. Disable push notifications by clicking Send desktop notifications

  8. Choose email preferences by selecting one of the three options:

    • Do Nothing: Notifications will never be sent via email

    • On Demand: A notification email will be sent when other notification methods are unsuccessful

    • Daily Digest: All missed notifications will be sent in a summative notification email the day after

  9. Disable Quiet Time preferences by deselecting the two options

    • Click Please do not send me any notifications between 6:00 PM and 9:00 AM to have notifications sent all hours of the day

    • Click Do not send me any notifications on weekends to have notifications sent all days of the week

  10. Click Save
