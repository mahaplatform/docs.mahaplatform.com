Events: Viewing the Performance of an EventÂ¶

Performance keeps a record of
To view this feature, you must have navigated to an event. Click here if you are having trouble reaching this point.

  1. Click :icon:`pie-chart`**Performance**

  2. Change time frame by clicking Last 30 Days, Last 60 Days, Year to Date, or Life to Date

  3. Hover over a data point to view the number of tickets purchased on a specific date

  4. View the numbers under Registrations, Tickets, Waiting List, and Revenue to view the number of customers who registered for an event, purchased a ticket, are on a waiting list, and the total amount of money made

  5. View the numbers or fractions (if there is/was a limit) to the right of the ticket types to see how many of each ticket type were sold
