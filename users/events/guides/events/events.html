Events: Interacting with an EventÂ¶

Events keeps a record of all events, current/upcoming and past, that have been organized by a program of your team.

  1. Click in the ribbon

  2. Click Events

  3. Click Events

  4. Click Upcoming Events to view events currently happening and events that are scheduled to occur in the future

  5. Click Past Events to view events that have ended

  6. Click and Clone event to make a duplicate of an event

  7. Click on an event to view an event and all information associated with the event, registration, tickets, etc.

  8. Under Event, you can view and edit the features of the event (Click the hyperlinks below for guides)
