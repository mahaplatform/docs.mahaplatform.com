Phone: Using the Call FeatureÂ¶

Calls keeps a record of calls to and from the numbers connected to programs of your organization.
To view details of a call or to contact the number again, follow the steps below.

  1. Click in the ribbon to pull up Phone in a side bar

  2. If not already on Calls, click Calls in the bottom ribbon

  3. Click the dropdown menu at the top to switch the phone number

  4. Click a call to view details

  5. Click to view the contact information associated with the phone number

    • Even if the number is unknown, all interactions connected to the phone number are still recorded

  6. Click to call the phone number

    • Choose to Call with cell phone or Call with Maha phone

  7. Once the recipient picks up, you will be offered three options:

    • Click transfer and select a user to transfer the call to

    • Click device to transfer the phone call to the other phone option (cell or Maha)

    • Click sms to send a send the recipient a text message

  8. Click to send a text message to the phone number

    • Compose the message how you would a comment
