Emails: Adding a Divider Block¶

The Divider block allows you to add a customizable line to separate other blocks in your email.
To use this block, you will need to have navigated to the email design tab. Click here if you are having trouble reaching this point.

  1. Place a Divider block in your email where desired

  2. By default, the divider will be a solid black line. If desired, change the properties of the divider

  3. Click Done
