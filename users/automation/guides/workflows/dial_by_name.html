Dial by Name Block¶

The Ask Question block is only available when designing a workflow for a voice campaign or when managing a workflow for a phone number .

  1. Place a Dial by Name block in your workflow where desired

  2. Click Speak text

    • Keep the greeting “Dial the first three letters of your party’s last name” or write your own

  3. Alternatively, click Play an audio file

  4. Click Add Recipient

  5. Again, you can click Speak text or Play an audio file

    • You can also click No announcement

  6. Click Choose a User and select a user

  7. Click Add to add the recipient

  8. If desired, click Add Recipient again

    • All added recipients will be called when an incoming call is routed to their extension - the call will be forwarded to the first recipient to answer

  9. Click Respond to hash (#) or Respond to star (*) if you would like recipients to be able to press the hash and star

  10. Click Done
