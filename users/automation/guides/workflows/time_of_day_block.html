Time of Day Block¶

The Time of Day block is available when designing a workflow for a voice campaign , an inbound SMS campaign , an outbound SMS campaign , or when managing a workflow for a phone number .
This block allows you to offer a certain path in the workflow when it is reached during a certain window of time (for example, 9AM to 5PM on weekdays) and another path for all other times (“else”).

  1. Place a Time of Day block in your workflow where desired

  2. Click Add Time Block

  3. Enter a name

  4. Select the desired day(s)

  5. Click Add Time

  6. Enter a start time

  7. Enter an end time (both these times will format to standard time and default to AM unless specified)

  8. Click Add

  9. To add another time of day, click Add Time again

  10. To delete a time of day, click

  11. To edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ a time of day, click

  12. Click Add

  13. To add another time block, click Add Time Block again

  15. To edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ a time block, click

  16. Click Done
