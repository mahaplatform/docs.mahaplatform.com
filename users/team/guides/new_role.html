Roles: Adding a New RoleÂ¶

To add a new role to your team, follow the steps below.

  1. Click in the ribbon

  2. Click Team

  3. Click Roles

  4. Click in the upper right hand corner

  5. Enter a title

  6. Click for each app to which people assigned this role will have access

    • If applicable, click to select the rights that users assigned the role will have

  7. Click Save
