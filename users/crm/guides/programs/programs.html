Programs: Interacting with a ProgramÂ¶

Programs keeps a record of all the programs that have been added by an organization.
Each program will have seven elements, which are hyperlinked below. Click to learn more about each element of the program.

  1. Click in the ribbon

  2. Click CRM

  3. Click Programs

  4. Click a program to view program information

  5. Click one of the seven tabs to navigate through the elements of a program. Click the hyperlinks for detailed guides

    • Details , Access , Senders , Lists , Topics , Templates , Properties

  6. Click to Edit Program or add/change/release the Provision Phone Number

  7. Add a new program
