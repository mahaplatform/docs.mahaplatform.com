ListsÂ¶

Lists displays the name of contact groups of a program.

  1. Click the in the ribbon

  2. Click CRM

  3. Click Programs

  4. Click a program

  5. Click Lists

  6. Click Add List

  7. Enter a list name

  8. Click Save

  9. Click to edit the list, create a workflow, or delete the list

  10. Click X Contacts to view the contacts that belong to the list

    • To add a contact to a list, refer to Adding a Contact to a List or a Program

  11. Click the tab entitled Automations to view workflows

  12. To view another template, click and choose the desired template
