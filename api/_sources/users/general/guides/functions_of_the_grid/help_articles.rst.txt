Functions of the Grid: Viewing a Help Article
=============================================

| The Maha Platform Help Center has a collection of help articles to help users navigate the platform and use its features.
| To access these articles, follow the steps below.

#. Click :icon:`question-circle` in the ribbon
#. Click **Articles**
#. `Search </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_search.html>`_ using key terms or scroll to find article
#. Click an article to view and follow steps as needed
#. Click hyperlinks to view further help articles
