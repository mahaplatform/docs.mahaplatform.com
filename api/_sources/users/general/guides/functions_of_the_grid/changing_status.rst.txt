Functions of the Grid: Changing the Status of an Item
=====================================================

| Sometimes an item will allow you to change its status, which lets others know what steps need to be taken or how the item has been interacted with.

#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v`
#. Click **Change Status**
#. Click the current status under **Status**
#. Select desired status (incomplete, pending, submitted, approved, rejected, reviewed, or exported)
#. Click the :icon:`angle-left`
#. Click **Save**
