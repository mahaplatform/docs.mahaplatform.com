Domains: Managing a Domain
==========================

| ????

#. Click the :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Websites**
#. Click **Domains**
#. Click a domain to view details, an audit trail, and comments
#. Click **DNS** to view the
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` to the right of the row and **Edit Record** to `edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ the record
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` to the right of the row, **Delete Record**, and **Yes** to delete the record
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` in the upper right hand corner and **Add Record** to add a new record

   * Enter a name
   * Select a type
   * Enter a TTL
   * Enter a value
   * Click **Save**
