Forms: Managing the SEO of a Form
=================================

| SEO is an acronym that stands for search engine optimization. ??? applies how?
| To manage the SEO of a form, you will need to have navigated to the settings of the form. Refer to `Form Settings </users/forms/guides/form_settings.html>`_ if you are having trouble reaching this point.

#. Click **SEO**
#. Enter a title
#. Enter a description
#. `Choose </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_upload_a_file.html>`_ image
#. Click **Done**
