Queues: Viewing a Queue
=======================

| NEED TO REVISIT

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Platform**
#. Click **Queues**
#. Click the number in the row entitled **Failed** of the desired queue to view
#. Click the number in the row entitled **Completed** of the desired queue to view
#. Click the number in the row entitled **Delayed** of the desired queue to view
#. Click a record within **Failed**, **Completed**, or **Delayed** to view details, the job, and any errors
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Retry Job** to manually retry the job

   * The job will automatically be ran three times
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Remove Job** to delete the job
