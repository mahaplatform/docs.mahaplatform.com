Viewing and Editing an Event
============================

| To view all the different parts of an event and to make changes to them you will need to have navigated to the event. Click `here </users/events/guides/events/events.html>`_ if you are having trouble reaching this point.

#. You will be brought to the :icon:`info-circle`**Details** page of the event you have selected

   * View event information, an audit trail, and comments
   * Click **View Public Registration** to open the event registration in a separate tab
   * Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Edit Event** to `edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ the event details or **Clone Event** to make a duplicate of an event
#. Click :icon:`calendar-o`**Sessions**

   * View event sessions
   * Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Edit Session** to `edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ the session
   * Click :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner to create a new session
#. Click :icon:`tags`**Ticket Types**

   * View ticket types
   * Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Edit Ticket Type** to `edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ the ticket type
   * Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **(De)activate Ticket Type** to perform associated action
   * Click :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner to create a new ticket type
#. Click :icon:`check-square`**Fields**

   * View contact fields and ticket fields by clicking **Contact** or **Ticket**
   * You can `edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ or delete a contact field by clicking :icon:`ellipsis-h` (these options are not available for the contact's first name, last name, or email)
   * You can add a contact field by clicking **Add Field** and add an `existing contact field </users/forms/guides/contact_fields.html>`_ or create and add a `custom field </users/forms/guides/custom_fields.html>`_
   * You can disable any ticket fields (besides name on ticket) by clicking :icon:`toggle-on`
   * You can add new ticket fields by clicking **Add field** and selecting an `option </users/forms/guides/custom_fields.html>`_
#. Click :icon:`cogs`**Automation**

   * Click :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner to `create a workflow </users/automation/guides/workflows/create_a_workflow.html>`_
   * Click on a `workflow </users/automation/guides/wokrflows/workflows.html>`_ to view details, emails, performance, edit the workflow, etc.
#. Click :icon:`bullhorn`**Campaigns** WRITE AFTER YOU DO CAMPAIGNS *********

   * Click :icon:`envelope`**Email**
   * Click :icon:`comment`**SMS**
   * Click :icon:`phone`**Voice**
#. Click :icon:`user-circle-o`**Registrations**

   * View contacts that registered for an event
   * Click a contact to view contacrt details, registration and revenue details, and ticket details **moreeeee*******
#. Click :icon:`tag`**Tickets**

   * View contacts that purchased a ticket
   * Click a contact to view a QR code, ticket holder details, event and attendance status
   * Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and click **View eTicket** to pull up a digital version of the contact's unique ticket in a separate tab

   * Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and click **Scan Tickets** to scan tickets at an event
#. Click :icon:`pie-chart`**Performance**
