Apps: Viewing an App
====================

| **Apps** has a list of all the apps to which your team has been given access on the Maha Platform.
| An organization can have access to any number of these apps, depending on what access was granted when the team was `created </users/platform/guides/new_team.html>`_ .
| Refer `here </users/platform/guides/apps.html>`_ to see a full list of the available apps.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Team**
#. Click **Apps**
#. Click an app to view the app icon, title, and description
