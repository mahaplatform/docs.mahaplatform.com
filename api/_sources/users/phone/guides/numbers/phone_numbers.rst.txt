Phone: Viewing Numbers
======================

| **Numbers** keeps a record of the phone numbers used for inbound calls to users and programs.
| Numbers can be provisioned and associated with multiple programs.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Phone**
#. Click **Numbers**
#. Click a number to view the phone number, associated program and workflow, the user who provisions the phone number, and comments
#. Click the **Manage Workflow** to `design the workflow </users/automation/guides/workflows/design_a_workflow.html>`_
