Expenses: Adding an Expense
===========================

| When you make a purchase with a work credit card or a store account, you should record this by adding a new expense.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Expenses**
#. Click **Items**
#. Click the :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner
#. Click **Expense**
#. Select date of purchase
#. Choose a vendor
#. Choose an account
#. `Upload </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_upload_a_file.html>`_ a receipt
#. Enter total
#. Enter tax
#. If desired, `add an allocation </users/finance/guides/add_an_allocation.html>`_
#. Click **Save**
