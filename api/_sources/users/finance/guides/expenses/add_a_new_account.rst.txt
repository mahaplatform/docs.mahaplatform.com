Adding a New Account
====================

.. note::

#. Click the :icon:`bars` **menu icon** in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Expenses**
#. Click **Accounts**
#. Click the :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner
#. Enter account name
#. Enter `vendor ID </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/display_elements.html>`_
#. Click **Save**
