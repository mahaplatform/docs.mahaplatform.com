Revenue: Viewing a Payment
==========================

| **Payments** acts as a record of all payments that have been made.
| You may also issue refunds to a customer on a payment.

#. Click the :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Revenue**
#. Click **Payments**
#. Click on a payment to view the payment details and the allocations of the payment
#. Click the hyperlinked 10-character string next to **Invoice** to view the payment `invoice </users/finance/guides/revenue/invoices.html>`_
#. Click the Braintree ID to open a new tab in Braintree
#. If applicable, click the PayPal ID open a new tab in PayPal
#. Click the hyperlinked date to view the `deposit </users/finance/guides/revenue/deposits.html>`_ in which the payment was included
#. Click Allocations to view how the transaction overhead cost had been allocated
#. To issue a refund of the payment, click the :icon:`ellipsis-v` and click `Issue Refund </users/finance/guides/revenue/issue_refund.html>`_
