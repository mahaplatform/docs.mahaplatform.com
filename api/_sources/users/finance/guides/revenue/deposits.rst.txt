Deposits
========
.. note::
   g

#. Click the :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Revenue**
#. Click **Deposits**
#. Click on a deposit to view the deposit details and transactions
#. Adding a new deposit:

   * Select a date
   * Select bank account
   * Select transaction(s). Mass select by clicking **select all*
   * Click **Save**
