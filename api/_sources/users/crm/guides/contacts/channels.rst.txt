Contacts: Viewing Channels of a Contact
=======================================

| **Channels** keeps a record of the interactions between a program and a contact.
| You may receive a message that reads "This contact has not given consent to send marketing related messages on this channel." Refer to `Managing the Consent of a Contact </users/crm/guides/contacts/consent.html>`_ to change a contact's consent.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **CRM*
#. Click **Contacts**
#. Click a contact
#. Click **Channels**
#. Click a program to view communication interactions between the program and contact
#. Click an interaction to view the message and status (e.g. delivered)
