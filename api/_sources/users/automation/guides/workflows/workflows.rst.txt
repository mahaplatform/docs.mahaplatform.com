Workflows
=========

| A workflow is a sequence of steps that will be triggered when an event occurs.

#. Click on a workflow to view status, details, audit trail, and comments
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and `Edit Workflow </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ to change the workflow's title
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and `Enroll Contacts </users/automation/guides/enroll_contacts.html>`_ to add contacts to a workflow
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Activate Workflow** or **Deactivate Workflow** to change the status of the workflow
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v`, **Delete Workflow**, and **Yes** to delete a workflow along with all of the emails and performance data associated with the workflow
#. Click the topic, list, form, etc. to pull up the trigger associated with the workflow
#. Click `Design Workflow </users/automation/guides/workflows/design_a_workflow.html>`_ to design your workflow
#. Click `Emails </users/automation/guides/emails/emails.html>`_ to view emails associated with the workflow, such as a confirmation email
#. Click `Performance </users/automation/guides/workflows/performance.html>`_ to view workflow enrollment data
