Inbound SMS Campaign Performance
================================

| **Performance** keeps a record of interactions with the inbound SMS campaign.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Campaigns**
#. Click **Inbound SMS**
#. Click on an inbound SMS campaign
#. Click **Performance**
#. Click the numbers under **Sessions**, **Active**, and **Converted** to see the contacts who enrolled in the workflow (initiated the campaign), are currently in the workflow, and were `converted </users/automation/guides/workflows/set_goal_block.html>`_

   * You can also view the percent of enrolled contacts who have been converted under **Conversion Rate**
#. Click the numbers to the right of each row to view the ??? who engaged in a certain action (the email was sent, opened, forwarded, etc.)??
