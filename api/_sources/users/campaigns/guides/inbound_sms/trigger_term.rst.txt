Inbound SMS: Trigger Terms Defined
=================================

| Trigger terms are an essential part of inbound messaging and the Maha Platform's `Inbound SMS Campaigns </users/campaigns/guides/inbound_sms/inbound_sms_campaigns.html>`_ .
| A trigger term is a keyword, a phrase, or a short code that is put out to the public or a target group of contacts.
| When an individual sends this trigger term to a designated number via text message, they have initiated an inbound messaging relationship.
| Trigger terms will have a prewritten response that will be sent in response upon reception of its associated trigger term.
