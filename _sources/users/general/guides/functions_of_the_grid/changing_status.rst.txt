Changing An Expenses Status
===========================

#. Click the <i class="fa fa-ellipsis-v"></i> **ellipsis icon**
#. Click **Change Status**
#. Click the current status under **Status**
#. Select desired status (incomplete, pending, submitted, approved, rejected, reviewed, or exported)
#. Click the <i class="fa fa-angle-left"></i> **back arrow**
#. Click **Save**
