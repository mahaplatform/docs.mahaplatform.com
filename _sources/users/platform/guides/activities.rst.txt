Activities: Viewing Activity History
====================================

| **Activities** keeps a chronological record of actions and interactions between users from **all** organizations of the Maha Platform (Refer `here </users/team/guides/activities.html>`_ to view only those of your organization).
| Each activity has a user and action-based description of the interaction.
| In addition to this description and the time the interaction took place, the organization and app icon with which the action is associated are displayed.

#. Click **Platform**
