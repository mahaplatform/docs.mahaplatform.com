Help: Creating a New Help Article
=================================

| To create a new help article, follow the steps below.
| When you save this help article, it will not yet be published. To publish it, refer `here </users/platform/guides/help.html>`_ .

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Platform**
#. Click **Help**
#. Click :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner
#. Enter a title
#. Click **Choose an app** and select the app to which the help article belongs
#. Choose videos to accompany the help article

   * Choose three videos for the different displays: Desktop (1024x768), desktop small (644x483), and mobile (279x483)
#. Enter the body of the help article
#. Click **Save**
