Banks: Viewing a Bank Account
=============================

| **Banks** displays all the bank accounts connected to organizations of the Maha Platform.
| Each bank account is associated with a team and displays a status (pending or active).
| Refer `here </users/finance/guides/revenue/bank_accounts.html>`_ to add a new bank account to an organization.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Platform**
#. Click **Banks**
#. Click a bank account to view the bank details
#. Click the hyperlinked BrainTree link to open the account in a separate BrainTree tab
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Edit Bank** to `edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ the bank account
