Help: Managing a Help Article
=============================

| **Help** is a compilation of all the `help articles that have been added </users/platform/guides/new_help_article.html>`_ to the Maha Platform.
| An article can belong to a specific app and have accompanying videos. These features will be displayed along with the title and publish status (marked by a :icon:`check` or :icon:`times`).

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Platform**
#. Click **Help**
#. Click on a help article to view details, video links, article, and comments
#. If available, click **watch video** to view the accompanying videos
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Edit Help Article** to `edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ the article
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Publish Article** to publish the article
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Unpublish Article** to unpublish the article

   * Alternatively, you can select the desired article(s) by clicking :icon:`circle-thin` to the left of the row and clicking **Publish Selected** or **Unpublish Selected**
   * You can mass select all the articles by clicking :icon:`circle-thin` in the upper left hand corner
