Drive: Creating a New Folder
============================

| To create a new folder, you must have navigated to your drive. Refer `here </users/drive/guides/managing_a_file.html>`_ if you are having trouble reaching this point.

#. Click **Create New Folder**
#. Enter the folder name
#. Click **Create**
#. Alternatively, click :icon:`ellipsis-v` in the upper right hand corner of **My Drive** and follow the above steps
