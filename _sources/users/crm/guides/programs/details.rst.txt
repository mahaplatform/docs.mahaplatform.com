Details
=======

| **Details** displays basic program information and an audit trail.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **CRM**
#. Click **Programs**
#. Click a program
#. Click **Details**
#. View the program's title, phone number, invoice address, bank account, and audit trail
#. You may `comment </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/comment.html>`_ on the program's detail page
