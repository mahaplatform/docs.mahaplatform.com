Report
======

.. note::
   **Report** displays a list of all the expenses being reported.

   The expenses are listed by date, and each expense displays other elements such as type, user, and description.

#. Click the <i class="fa fa-bars"></i> **menu icon** in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Expenses**
#. Click **Report**
#. Click on the expense to view receipt upload, elements, audit trail, and comments
#. Click **Download** or **Export** to download or print image of the expense receipt
#. To change status:

   * Click the <i class="fa fa-ellipsis-v"></i> **ellipsis icon** in the top right hand corner of the page
   * Click **Change Status**
   * Click the current status
   * Click the desired status
   * Click the <i class="fa fa-angle-left"></i> **back arrow**
   * Click **Save**
#. If desired, leave a `comment </users/finance/guides/add_an_allocation.html>`_
#. If desired, hide a certain element by clicking the <i class="fa fa-angle-down"></i> *arrow icon**, then clicking on the element
#. If desired, `export </users/finance/guides/export_records.html>`_ reports

   * To export one expense or a select few, click the <i class="fa fa-circle-thin"></i> **circle icon** to the left of the row for each item
   * Click the pop-up tab entitled **Export X item(s)**
   * Click **Yes**
   * Select date to create batch
   * Click **Save**
#. If desired, `edit </users/finance/guides/how_to_edit.html>`_ expenses
