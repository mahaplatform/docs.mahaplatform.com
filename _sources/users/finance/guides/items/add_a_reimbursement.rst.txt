Add a Reimbursement
===================

.. note::
   When a user makes a purchase with their own money for work-related expenses that should be reimbursed, they should record this by adding a new reimbursement.

#. Click the <i class="fa fa-bars"></i> **menu icon** in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Expenses**
#. Click **Items**
#. Click the <i class="fa fa-plus"></i> **plus icon** in the upper right hand corner
#. Click **Reimbursement**
#. Select date of purchase
#. Choose a vendor
#. `Upload </users/finance/guides/how_to_upload_a_file.html>`_ a receipt
#. Enter total
#. Click **Save**
