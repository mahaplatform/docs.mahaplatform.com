Revenue: Creating a New Invoice
===============================

| To create a `new invoice, follow the steps below.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Revenue**
#. Click **Invoices**
#. Click :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner
#. Choose a program
#. Choose a customer
#. Select the current date
#. Select invoice due date
#. Add notes
#. Click **Add Line Item**

     * Choose a project
     * Choose a revenue type
     * Describe the item
     * Enter item unit price
     * Enter tax rate
     * Enter quantity
     * If item is tax deductible, check box
     * Click **Done**
#. Add more line items if necessary
#. Click **Save**
