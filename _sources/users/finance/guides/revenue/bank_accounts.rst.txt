Adding a Bank Account
=====================

.. note::
   g

#. Click the :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Finance**
#. Click **Revenue**
#. Click **Bank Accounts**
#. Click the :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner
#. Enter a title
#. Enter the bank's routing number
#. Enter your bank account number
#. Enter the bank code
#. Click **Save**
