Expenses: Item Statuses Defined
===============================

| The **Status** of an item indicates where it is in the submission process.
| To manually change the status of an item, you will need to have navigated to a **Choose Status** pop-up tab. Refer `here </users/finance/guides/expenses/items.html>`_ if you are having trouble reaching this point.

#. Incomplete: The item still has fields that are required but not filled out

   * Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and click **Edit Item** to add the missing fields marked with **REQUIRED**
#. Pending:  The item is ready for submission but has not yet been submitted
#. Submitted: The item has been submitted for approval but has yet to be approved or rejected
#. Approved: The submitted item was approved
#. Rejected: The submitted item was rejected
#. Reviewed: The submitted item was reviewed but not yet approved or rejected
#. Exported: The item was `exported </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/exporting_records.html>`_
