Ask Question Block
==================

| The **Ask Question** block is only available when designing a workflow for a `voice campaign </users/campaigns/guides/voice/voice_campaigns.html>`_ or when managing a workflow for a `phone number </users/phone/guides/numbers/phone_numbers.html>`_ .

#. Place a **Ask Question** block in your workflow where desired
#. Enter a question
#. Click **Done**
#. Click **Add Answer**
#. Select **equals**, **does not equal**, **contains**, or **does not contain** (click `here </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/criteria_options.html>`_ for definitions)
#. Enter a word or phrase
#. Click **Add**
#. To add another answer, click **Add Answer** again
#. To delete an answer, click :icon:`times`
#. To edit </users/general/guides/functions_of_the_grid/how_to_edit.html>`_ an answer, click :icon:`pencil`
#. Click **Done**
