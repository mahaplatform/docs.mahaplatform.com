Settings: Viewing the Settings of a Team
========================================

| **Settings** displays the logo, title, name, and invoice address of a team.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Team**
#. Click **Settings** to view `team </users/platform/guides/teams.html>`_ details
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Edit Settings** to `edit </users/general/guides/teams.html>`_ the team's settings
