Phone: Viewing Text Messages
============================

| **SMS** keeps a record of the text messages that happen through the Maha Platform.
| It keeps track of the number the text message was **To** and **From** as well as the direction of the message.
| The date and time the message was sent/received is also displayed along with the media, price, and message status.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Team**
#. Click **Phone**
#. Click **SMS**
