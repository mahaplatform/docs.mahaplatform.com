Roles: Adding a New Role
========================

| To add a `new role </users/team/guides/roles.html>`_ to your team, follow the steps below.

#. Click :icon:`bars` in the ribbon
#. Click **Team**
#. Click **Roles**
#. Click :icon:`plus` in the upper right hand corner
#. Enter a title
#. Click :icon:`toggle-off` for each app to which people assigned this role will have access

   * If applicable, click :icon:`circle-thin` to select the rights that users assigned the role will have
#. Click **Save**
