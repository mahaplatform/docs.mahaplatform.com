Stores: Managing the Inventory of a Store
=========================================

| **Inventory** keeps track of how much stock there is left of each product in a store.
| To manage the inventory of a store, you will need to have navigated to the store. Refer to `Stores </users/stores/guides/stores.html>`_ if you are having trouble reaching this point.

#. Click :icon:`cubes` **Inventory**
#. View, per product, the number of items that are:

   * In stock: these items are in the warehouse
   * Reserved: these items are currently in someone's cart
   * Unfulfilled: these items have been ordered but not yet fulfilled
   * On hand: these items are available for sale
#. Click :icon:`ellipsis-v` and **Update Inventory** to change the number of items in stock

   * Click :icon:`times`
   * Enter a new number
   * Repeat for each change needed
   * Click **Save**
